The Self Protection Movement (S.P.M) orders each citizen to stay at home as much as possible. This is an order from above to all of you. Obey and follow the S.P.M laws and no charges will be put on you. We have to deal with this new reality to protect us but also our loved ones. Our world has become arbitrary and this is the new normal life. Do not travel for leisure, only for work. If possible work from home, avoid working outside or somewhere else then your own house or apartment. Grow your food in your garden if possible or have access to an open public garden. Travel environmental friendly with a bike. Avoid animal products and avoid waste. Eat local or only at home. Furthermore, it is required by law to wear a full-body suit and mask for your protection and others. Follow these steps to slow down the spread of the virus and to slow down globalisation. Do it for yourself, do it for the community and especially for our planet.
